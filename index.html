📋 Rules & Scoring

🎯 How to Play

Goal: Listen to the video and click on the map where you think this language is from!

⏱️ Timing

• You have 45 seconds per video
• Timer starts when video begins playing
• No penalty for slow internet - timer waits for video to load

🏆 Scoring System

Correct Country: Up to 5,000 points

• Base score: 4,000 points
• Speed bonus:
  ⚡ 0-10 seconds: +1,000 points
  🔥 11-20 seconds: +500 points
  ⭐ 21-30 seconds: +200 points
  📍 31-45 seconds: +0 bonus

Wrong Country: Up to 2,000 points based on distance

• Closer guess = more points
• Same continent ≈ 200-800 points
• Different continent ≈ 0-200 points

🎮 Game Format

• 5 rounds per game
• Final score = sum of all rounds
• Maximum possible score: 25,000 points

🌍 Modes

Classic Mode: Pure challenge - no country labels
Chill Mode: Country names shown on map
